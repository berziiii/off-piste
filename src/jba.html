JBA Financial Advisors

JBA Financial Advisors needed a redesign of their corporate website to connect their current and potential customers to the brand. The redesign was focused on bringing JBA into the modern digital era with a clean, crisp and efficient site design. It included a responsive design for mobile viewing and utilized an effective approach to conceal content rich sections that are made visible on deliberate user interaction.

UX, User Interface Design, HTML5, CSS3, Bootstrap, Sass, Grunt, JavaScript, jQuery
